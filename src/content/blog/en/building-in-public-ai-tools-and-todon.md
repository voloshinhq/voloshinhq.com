I am rebuilding my website and turning it into a small public base for everything I am learning and shipping.

The direction is simple: practical AI tools, infrastructure experiments, and small products that can be used by people who work on laptops and desktops every day.

One of the ideas is Todon: a service inspired by to-do and done. The point is not to announce a giant startup before anything works. The point is to make progress visible, ship small pieces, and keep the loop honest.

This site is part of that loop.

Less noise. More shipping.
